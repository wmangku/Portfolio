PROJECTS

Project 1: Techionista Holiday

May 12, 2022

Techionista Holiday is a e-commerce company for travelling. Techionista Holiday’s mission is to make it easy for travellers to organize the perfect trip. On their website, you can book hotel rooms, apartments and other unique stays. As a traveller, you can give reviews and comments on your stay.

Assignment: Techionista Holiday managers would love to gain more insight into different types of travelers and their feedback. These valuable insights can help them improve Techionista Holiday’s website and their capability of catering to their users’ needs.

Dataset: The dataset contains 515,000 records and has 17 fields, such as customer reviews, customer scoring and hotel information.

Step by step plan

  • Connect to data
  • Extract data
  • Transform and clean data
  • Load data
  • Analyzing data
  • Researching trends and patterns
  • Visualizing data
  • Presenting the results
